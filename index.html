Taj Mahal (India)

A white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, symbolizing eternal love.

Great Wall of China

A massive wall stretching over 13,000 miles, built to protect ancient China from invasions and raids.

Machu Picchu (Peru)

A well-preserved Inca citadel nestled high in the Andes Mountains, showcasing advanced ancient architecture.

Roman Colosseum (Italy)

A grand amphitheater in Rome, hosting gladiatorial battles and events in ancient Roman times.

Christ the Redeemer (Brazil)

A 98-foot-tall statue of Jesus Christ atop Mount Corcovado in Rio de Janeiro, symbolizing peace and Christianity.

Chichen Itza (Mexico)

A large Mayan archaeological site featuring the iconic El Castillo pyramid, reflecting astronomical precision.

Petra (Jordan)

An ancient city carved into rose-red sandstone cliffs, known for its archaeological marvels like the Treasury.
